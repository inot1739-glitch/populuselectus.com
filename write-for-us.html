What We're Looking For

Topics we cover: Mindset & psychology, productivity systems, career growth, financial literacy, habits & behavior change.
Article length: 1,000 – 2,500 words. Long enough to be useful, short enough to respect the reader's time.
Style: Research-backed, practical, conversational. No fluff. Every article must include actionable takeaways.
Originality: Content must be 100% original and not published elsewhere. No AI-generated filler.

What We Don't Accept

❌ Promotional or sales content · ❌ Content already published elsewhere · ❌ Topics outside our 4 pillars · ❌ Articles under 1,000 words · ❌ Poor grammar or structure
